# Restaurant Order Analysis

## Overview 
SQ analysis of a restaurant database to find ordering patterns, top items, and revenue insights.

## Questions Answered 
1. What do our menu items and orders look ike?
2. What are the least and most ordered items?
3. What are the top 5 highest spend orders?
4. What categories dominate the highest orders?
5. Whaat insights can we draw from top spenders?

## 
Key Findings
- Chips and Salsa = most ordered
- Chicken Tacos = least ordered
- Italian food dominates high spend orders
-  Recommendation: Keep Italian items, reconsider Chicken Tacos
